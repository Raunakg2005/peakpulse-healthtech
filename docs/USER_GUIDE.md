3. **Enter Duration**: Minutes spent on activity
4. **Choose Intensity**:
   - Light (0.7x multiplier)
   - Moderate (1.0x - default)
   - Vigorous (1.3x multiplier)

5. **See Estimated Calories**: Real-time calculation based on your weight
6. **Submit**: Click "Log Activity"

### Understanding Calorie Calculations

PeakPulse uses **MET (Metabolic Equivalent of Task)** values:

**Formula**: `Calories = MET √ó Weight(kg) √ó Duration(hours) √ó Intensity`

**Example:**
- Running (MET = 9.8)
- Weight: 70kg
- Duration: 30 minutes (0.5 hours)
- Intensity: Moderate (1.0x)
- **Result**: 9.8 √ó 70 √ó 0.5 √ó 1.0 = **343 calories**

### Viewing Activity History

- Today's activities appear in the calorie tracker

- Verify you meet badge criteria
- Some badges have hidden requirements
- Progress updates after each activity

### ML Predictions Not Showing?
- Ensure ML service is running (port 8000)
- Check that models are trained
- Predictions need at least 7 days of data
